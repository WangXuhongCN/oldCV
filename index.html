About me

I was born in May 1995, and I am currently a 3rd-year PhD candidate at School of Electronic Information and Electrical Engineering, Shanghai Jiao Tong University, advised by Prof. Yupu Yang. I acquired my bachelor degree from School of Electronics and Information Engineering, Sichuan University in 2017.

My research lies at applying variational inference, (graph) neural networks and anomaly detection techniques to cyber-physical systems and industrial complex networks.
